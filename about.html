Our Philosophy

We believes that fashion plays a defining role in enhancing a person’s confidence and sense of self. We seek to create luxurious designs that leave unforgettable impressions on the world, and are yet versatile enough to transcend people, places and occasions.

We pay homage to the supreme craftsmanship of Nepal by creating exquisite ensembles and handcrafted jewellery, oftentimes designing collections with the sole aim of uplifting our artisans. GSK Versatile stands for a simple, unembellished thought—to create beautiful tomorrows for the planet and its people.

The Founder

From an early age, Goma Tiwari knew she wanted to helm her own fashion house. Having spent her summer holidays growing up in Simra, and later armed with a degree in Fashion Design, Goma’s influences were apparent right from her first collection. Her signature style showed an affinity for indigenous craft tradition, coupled with a modern aesthetic that soon made Goma Tiwari one of Nepal’s best fashion designers.
